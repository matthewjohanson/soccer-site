5 Drills Every U12 Coach Should Know

By Coach Matt

Getting your players excited about practice starts with drills that are fun and game-like. Here are five that work every time with U10 and U12 players.

1. Triangle Passing

Set up a triangle with cones. Players pass and move to a new cone. This teaches spacing and timing.

2. 4v1 Rondo

Four players form a square and one goes in the middle. The goal is quick passing to keep possession. Great for reaction time and communication.

3. Cone Gate Dribbling

Scatter cones in pairs to form gates. Players dribble through as many gates as possible in 30 seconds. It improves ball control under pressure.

4. Quick Turns Challenge

Set up four stations where players must perform a turn (Cruyff, drag back, inside hook, etc.). Time them and encourage improvement each round.

5. 3v3 to Goal

Small-sided games encourage decision-making. Keep it fast and fun with rotating teams or "winner stays on."
